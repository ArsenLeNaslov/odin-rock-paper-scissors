This game can be played entirely on the browser's Developer Console! To open it:

  1. Right-click with your mousepad (or mouse) on any area of the page, and select "Inspect"
  2. Navigate to the "Console" tab.
  3. Follow the prompts in the console to play the game.

Alternatively, depending on your OS, you can use the below keyboard shortcuts to open the Developer Console:

  • Windows Edge and Linux: Ctrl + Shift + J
  • Mac Safari: Command + Option + J
  • Google Chrome: Ctrl + Alt + I

Once the game is finished, refresh the page to play again, or type playGame() and Enter in the console to restart the game.
